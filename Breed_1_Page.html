Information

Ongole is a prominent dual purpose breed of Andhra Pradesh. The breed got its name from its geographical area of origin, i.e. Ongole. The breed is also known as “Nellore” as the Ongole area was earlier in Nellore district.The breed is known for hardiness, disease resistance and capacity to thrive on scanty resources.
