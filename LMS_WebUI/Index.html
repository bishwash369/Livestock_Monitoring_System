Tracking Livestock

Livestock Monitoring System is a prototype device designed to monitor livestock animals by tracking their location and monitoring their health with constraints such as body temperature and humidity

Cost-Efficient

This system provides millions of a cost efficient system which prevents small scale poultry and livestock farmers from spending thousands of dollars on a monitoring system
